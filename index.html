In our urban houses what should we do without cats? in our

sitting or bedrooms,our libraries and storerooms, our farms,

barns,and rickyards,in our docks, our granaries,our ships and our

wharves,in our corn markets,and other places too numerous

to mention,how useful they are! in our ships,however,the rats off set them

at defiance; still they are of great service.
